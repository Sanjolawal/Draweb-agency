The One Million Mission:

Teach one Million people how to make a living, doing what they love (without losing their soul).
To make programming equitable for anyone, be it students or teachers!.
And create tools that makes life easier and friendly to use, at a reduced price.

One Million Seems impossible right?, Yes we understand.

"Problems are not solved, with same thoughts, when finding solution." — Albert Einstein.

Institutions are slow to adaptation. Committees are where good ideas and innovative thinking go to die. For change. We need to wipe the slate clean and begin with bold and radical thinking.

We have a simple idea—to put money, attention and resources where it's most effective: in front of young people. Not in administrators, buildings, parking lots, or monuments. Bring people together to form a vibrant community and safe space to share, discuss and learn. Connect people from different cultures, interests, age groups and life experiences.

Recruiting the best, and allow them to profit from their ideas. That sounds modern, disruptive, and unlike any school out there! That’s because we believe the future of learning isn't a school at all. classrooms have no walls, students are never late, and classes are always in session.

Welcome to Draweb— Reducing learning and life stress, by building budget friendly profitable tools
